Untangled Worlds: Seminar Series

A new seminar series about untangling real-world complex systems

Our series will bring together researchers and practitioners whose work engages with interconnected, adaptive, or emergent phenomena in real-world settings, from public health and urban resilience to economic inequality and environmental change.

We're interested in work which connects to policy, practice, or public life, regardless of disciplinary home.

Whether you're in academia, government, or the NGO sector, we would love for you to be involved.
